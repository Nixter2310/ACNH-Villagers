Welcome to Glimmer!

Like a lot of people out there during lockdown I purchased a Nintendo Switch and the first game I bought was Animal Crossing: New Horizons. I was already a fan of the franchise as I had previously played Animal Crossing: Wild World on my Nintendo DS. During that difficult time it was nice to be able to escape to my own little island and use my imagination to build something fun!
